Child Safety Standards — Social Battery

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Social Battery ("the app") is committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users and to complying with all applicable child safety laws and regulations.

Age Requirements

Social Battery is intended for users aged 18 and older. The app is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly permit children under 13 to use the app. If we become aware that a user is under 13, we will take steps to remove their account and associated data.

No User-Generated Public Content

Social Battery does not host public user-generated content. All status messages and battery levels are shared only between users who have mutually connected by exchanging private invite codes. There are no public profiles, public feeds, or open chat features.

Prevention of Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

Social Battery has a zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). Any content or behavior that exploits or endangers minors is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate account termination and reporting to the appropriate authorities, including the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) where required by law.
